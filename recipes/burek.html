Ingredients

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Stack two sheets phyllo dough on a work surface
  3. Coil the tube into a snail shape and place onto an ungreased baking sheet
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 20 to 30 minutes.
