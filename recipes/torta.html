Torta

Home

Description

This torta rustica is served at room temperature. Two teaspoons dried basil may be substituted for fresh basil. Use ham or prosciutto.

Ingredients

  • Cornmeal
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Ham

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 F (190 C). Set aside a 9-inch springform pan.
  2. Meanwhile, stir ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, crumbled feta, 1 egg, parsley, basil, and oregano together in a bowl until combined
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until crust is deep golden brown and pulls away from sides, about 1 hour.
  4. Cool in pan on wire rack for 45 minutes. Remove pan sides; cool completely. Cut into 12 wedges. Serve at room temperature
